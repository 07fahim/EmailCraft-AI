Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about EmailCraft AI

How does the AI generate personalized emails?

Our system uses 6 specialized AI agents that work together. It analyzes the job posting, profiles the recipient's persona, retrieves relevant templates and portfolio items, generates a personalized email, and then evaluates and optimizes it for maximum impact.

Can I use my own email templates?

Yes! You can upload your own templates to the system. Our RAG-powered retrieval system will learn from them and use them as references for generating new emails.

How long does it take to generate an email?

Most emails are generated in under 30 seconds. For batch processing, we apply intelligent rate limiting to avoid API limits. Expect about 30 seconds per email in production mode.

What's the quality scoring system?

We evaluate emails on 5 metrics: Clarity (is it easy to understand?), Tone (is it professional?), Length (is it concise?), Personalization (is it tailored?), and Spam Risk (will it get filtered?). Each metric is scored 0-10.

How does lead generation work?

Our lead generation feature scrapes businesses from Google Maps based on your criteria (e.g., "software companies in San Francisco") and automatically generates personalized cold emails for each lead. Export all results to Excel with one click. You can add contact emails manually or use external email finder tools.

Can I process emails in bulk?

Absolutely! Upload a CSV file with job URLs or role/industry combinations, and our system will generate emails for all of them. You can process 10-200+ emails at once and export results to Excel.

Is my data secure?

Yes, we take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We use industry-standard security practices and never share your data with third parties.
